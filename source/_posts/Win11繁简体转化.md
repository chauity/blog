# Win11繁简体转化

## 缘由

windows的繁简体默认转化快捷键是 ```ctrl + shift + f```,会与一些ide的全局搜索快捷键冲突。

于是我就把快捷键给关了，现在想繁体输出，去网上百度了下，本文记录下win11如何开关快捷键。



## 步骤

#### 1.打开设置

#### 2.点击时间和语言

![image-20231221151748665](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211517486658.png)

#### 3.点击语言和区域

![image-20231221151852970](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211518529703.png)

#### 4.点击中文(简体,中国)右边的三个点，弹出框点击语言选项

![image-20231221152021012](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211520210127.png)

#### 5.拉到最底，点击微软拼音右边三个点，弹出框点击键盘选项

![image-20231221152313678](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211523136786.png)

#### 6.点击按键

![image-20231221152418898](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211524188985.png)

#### 7.拉到最底，找到热键繁简体切换

![image-20231221152500238](https://gcore.jsdelivr.net/gh/chauity/images/typora_imgs/image-202312211525002384.png)
